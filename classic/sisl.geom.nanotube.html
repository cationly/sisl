sisl.geom.nanotube.nanotube(bond, atom=None, chirality=(1, 1))¶

Create a nano-tube geometry

This routine is implemented as in ASE with few changes.

Parameters:

bond: float

length between atoms in nano-tube

atom: Atom(6)

nanotube atoms

chirality: (int, int)

chirality of nanotube (n, m)
